Account Deletion – Jaan

Users can request deletion of their account at any time.

How to request deletion

Please email support@yourdomain.com from the address used to sign in (Google OAuth), and include the subject line Account deletion request.

What will be deleted

  • Google account identifier
  • Email address
  • Any app-related account data stored on our systems (if applicable)

Data retention

We permanently delete personal data within 30 days of receiving the request. We do not sell personal data and do not share it with third parties except where required to provide the service (e.g., authentication providers).
