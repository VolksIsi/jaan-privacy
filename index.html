Privacy Policy

Jaan – Personal AI Agent
Developed by Jaan Intelligence
Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

Jaan is a personal AI agent app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

Jaan uses your device's microphone to process voice commands. Voice input is processed in real time and is not stored permanently on our servers.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal identification information. We do not share any data with third parties. We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs.

4. Microphone Permission

Jaan requires microphone access solely to enable voice control. This permission is used only while the app is active and a command is being given.

5. Internet Access

Jaan connects to the internet to execute tasks on your behalf (e.g. web browsing, search). No personal data is transmitted beyond what is necessary to fulfill your request.

6. Children's Privacy

Jaan is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
